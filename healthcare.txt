Comman Illness Symptoms Guide

Flu:
Symptoms : fever,headache,bodypains,fatigue,cough.
Recommendation:rest,drink fluids,consult a doctor if fever persists.

Common Cold : 
Symptoms : sneezing , runny nose, sore throat, mild cough.
Recommendation: rest,warm fluids, over-the counter medicine.

Food poisoning:
Symptoms : stomach pain  ,vomiting, diarrhea, nausea.
Recommendation : drink fluids, rest , seek medical help if severe.

Migrane : 
Symptoms : severe headache , sensitivity to light ,nausea.
Recommendation: rest in dark room , pain relief medicine.

Covid 19 : 
Symptoms : fever , cough , loss of smell,breathing difficulty.
Recommendation : isolate and consult healthcare provider.
